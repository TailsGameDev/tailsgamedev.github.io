Privacy Policy for Cards On Adventure

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

Thank you for playing Cards On Adventure!

1. Data Collection

We believe in providing a simple and private gaming experience. Cards On Adventure does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its players. We do not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable data.

2. Third-Party Services

Cards On Adventure does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect personal data. The game operates entirely offline and does not connect to any external servers for analytics, advertising, or social media features.

3. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are in full compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations around the world. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy here. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at tailsgamedev@gmail.com.
